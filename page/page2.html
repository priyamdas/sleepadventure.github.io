Day 1

You get into the office at the usual time and settle into your work.

During your lunch break, you come across an article online about the importance of sleeping well. It mentions things like having a strengthened immune system, better memory recall, and even greater creativity at work if you sleep about 8-9 hours a night. However, if you skimp on sleep for even one night, that's sleep that can never be "made up" another night.

You also note with some interest that people who regularly get enough sleep are perceived as more attractive than those who don't, giving new meaning to "beauty sleep."
