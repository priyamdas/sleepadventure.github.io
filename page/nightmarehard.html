** Fun Fact: A study done in 1924 by Jenkins & Dalenbach found that after many hours of wakefulness there is catastrophic forgetting of things that you've recently learned. Just 2 hours of sleep is enough to stagnate this forgetfulness. However even partial sleep deprivation over a period of time can be just as bad as total sleep deprivation (Van Dongen et al. 2003).
